# Data Dictionary

| Variable | Description |
|---|---|
| `sector` | Economic sector name |
| `sector_code` | Short sector identifier |
| `final_demand` | Final demand for sector output |
| `total_output` | Total output required after inter-industry linkages |
| `shock_output` | Total output under demand shock |
| `output_change` | Change in total output relative to baseline |
| `consumption_share` | Share of total output allocated to private consumption |
| `investment_share` | Share of total output allocated to future productive capacity |
| `public_provision_share` | Share of total output allocated to public goods and collective provision |
| `restoration_share` | Share of total output allocated to maintenance, repair, and ecological/social reproduction |
| `wage_share` | Share of sector output paid to labor |
| `profit_share` | Share of sector output accruing to capital/profit/rent |
| `ecological_intensity` | Stylized ecological pressure per unit of output |
| `employment_intensity` | Stylized employment requirement per unit of output |
| `produced_capital` | Produced capital stock in reproduction simulation |
| `natural_capital` | Ecological/natural capital stock in reproduction simulation |
| `depreciation_rate` | Produced capital depreciation rate |
| `depletion_rate` | Natural capital depletion rate |
| `regeneration_rate` | Natural capital regeneration rate |
