# Methodology

This repository treats economic systems as embedded, historical, institutional, ecological, and political systems.

The workflow uses structured metadata for:

- economic concepts
- economic traditions
- article planning
- microeconomic and macroeconomic domains
- political economy
- development economics
- ecological economics
- beyond-GDP indicators
- source hierarchy

The method combines economics, political economy, institutional analysis, ecological economics, development studies, public finance, systems thinking, and sustainability research.
