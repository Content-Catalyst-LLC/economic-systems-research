# Source Hierarchy

## Tier 1: Official and Statistical Sources

- OECD reports and indicator frameworks
- World Bank reports and datasets
- IMF topic pages and policy diagnostics
- UN sustainable development reports
- UNEP circularity and economic transformation guidance
- National statistical agencies and official national accounts

## Tier 2: Scholarly and Technical Sources

- Peer-reviewed economic research
- University press books
- Economic history scholarship
- Ecological economics literature
- Development economics literature
- Public finance and welfare economics literature

## Tier 3: Applied and Critical Sources

- Policy reports
- Civil-society economic analysis
- Think tank papers
- Well-being indicator frameworks
- Labor and inequality reports
- Transition and resilience case studies
